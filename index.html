Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account with us is easy! Simply click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button located at the top right corner of the website. Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and password, and then click "Create Account." Once your account is created, you can start enjoying a personalized shopping experience, track your orders, and receive exclusive offers.

We offer a range of secure payment options to ensure a hassle-free checkout process. You can pay using major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also accept payments through popular digital wallets like PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay. For certain regions, we provide the option of bank transfers. Rest assured, all transactions are encrypted to protect your financial information.

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your package. Additionally, you can log in to your account and navigate to the "Order History" section to track all your past and current orders conveniently.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not happy with your order, you can initiate a return within 30 days of receiving the product. Please ensure the item is in its original condition with all packaging intact. Once we receive the return, we will process your refund or exchange according to our refund policy.

We strive to process and ship orders as quickly as possible. Generally, orders are processed within 1-2 business days from the time of purchase. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen during checkout. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping options may be available for quicker delivery.

You can contact our customer support team through various channels. You can send us an email at support@yourwebsite.com or use the "Contact Us" form on our website. Additionally, you can call our helpline at +92-7485994.
